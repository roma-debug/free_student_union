ABOUT FSU

FSU is a student-led and independent organization that exists to represent the student at purwanchal campus(dharan) of T.U . Our main purpose is to represent the best interest of all students on the issue they may face.whether that is arrised locally on campus or nationally. fsu is established for the welfare and development of student .it develops leadership skill in student by making them engaged in various academic ,social cultural activities organized by fsu. we aimed to improvise skill of students so we organise games,competitive programs,cultural programs etc among faculty students to cultivate knowledge of sports, technology and culture. the first major motive for joining college is education but there are various other matter thet affect their process of getting an education.there are many problems related to admossion,hostel fees,irresponsible teacher and lack of infrastructure in college which students are unable to solve individually.fsu is always there to address such problems. polititics and leadership is foundation of fsu. it is the main base of practicing politics in college .it builds discipline between the college students and helps them solving problems by unifying all of them together .fsu offers linkage between student and external corporate which helps them to take part in national and corporate affarirs . FSU can help in lots of ways whether that is personal or acedemic studies. FSU is also a great place to go is you have an idea of how things could be improved at campus.
